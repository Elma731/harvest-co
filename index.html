Our Story

A Neighborhood Brunch Spot

Harvest & Co. grew from a simple idea: that the best meals are the ones shared slowly, with people you love, in a room that feels like home. We source our ingredients from local farms each morning, letting the season guide what lands on your plate.

Tucked into the corner of a sun-filled building, our dining room is a gathering place for the neighborhood. It could be first dates, lazy Sundays, and everything in between. Come as you are. Stay as long as you like.
